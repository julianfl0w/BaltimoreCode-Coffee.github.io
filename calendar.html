Baltimore Code & Coffee Demo Day

Date and Time:

2024-09-28 11:00:00 EST

Location

FastForward U, Johns Hopkins University

Description

Our Demo Day is a chance for members of the local tech community to show off the cool projects they’ve been working on. Come and enjoy our four participant’s ten minute demos with 5 minutes for questions. After the demos, each of the participants will have their own table for you to ask more questions about their work in depth. After 30 mins of this, we will have a vote for the winner. **SCHEDULE:** Networking - 11:00am-11:30am Demos - 11:30am-12:30pm Table Talk - 12:30pm-1:00pm Vote 1:00pm-1:10pm Networking - 1:10pm - 2:00pm **Venue:** The Pava Center for Entrepreneurship ( formerly known as FastForwardU) **PARKING:** If you are driving to visit us, we suggest parking in the R House overflow lot that is catty corner from R House across W. 29th St. It’s shared with the Baltimore Police Department, and free to park there.

Location

FastForward U, Johns Hopkins University

Description
